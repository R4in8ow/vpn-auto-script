# 📖 x-ui VPN Configuration Guide (Client Setup)

This guide will help you configure the 4 main protocols (VLESS+Reality, VLESS+CDN, Hysteria 2, and Shadowsocks) inside the **3x-ui Panel** after successfully running the installation script.

---

## 1. VLESS + Reality (Recommended for Speed & Stability)
Reality is the most secure protocol that masks your traffic as regular website traffic without needing a CDN.

**Steps in 3x-ui Panel:**
1. Go to **Inbounds** -> Click `+ Add Inbound`.
2. **Remark:** `Reality_Node` (or any name).
3. **Protocol:** `vless`.
4. **Port:** `7443` (Ensure this port is open in your firewall).
5. **Client -> Email:** `user1` (or any name).
6. Go to the **Stream Settings** tab.
7. **Network:** `tcp`.
8. **Security:** `reality`.
9. **uTLS:** `chrome`.
10. **Target (Dest):** `web.dev:443` or `www.google.com:443` (This is the site you are masking as).
11. **SNI:** `web.dev` or `www.google.com` (Must match the Target).
12. Click **Get New Cert** to automatically generate `Public Key` and `Private Key`.
13. Click **Get New Seed** to generate `Short IDs`.
14. Click **Create** to save.

> **Important:** Your Panel Domain MUST be **Proxy OFF (Grey Cloud)** in Cloudflare for Reality to work.

---

## 2. VLESS + CDN (For Bypassing Strict Blocks)
This protocol routes your traffic through Cloudflare's CDN, making it very hard to block, but it might be slightly slower than Reality.

**Steps in 3x-ui Panel:**
1. Go to **Inbounds** -> Click `+ Add Inbound`.
2. **Remark:** `CDN_Node`.
3. **Protocol:** `vless`.
4. **Port:** `459` (or `2083`, must be a Cloudflare supported port).
5. **Client -> Email:** `user1`.
6. Go to the **Stream Settings** tab.
7. **Network:** `ws` (WebSocket).
8. **Path:** `/vless` (or any custom path like `/cdn`).
9. **Security:** `tls`.
10. **SNI:** `cdn.yourdomain.com` (Your CDN Domain).
11. **Certificate:** Use the paths generated by the script:
    * **Public Key Path:** `/root/cert/cdn.yourdomain.com/fullchain.pem`
    * **Private Key Path:** `/root/cert/cdn.yourdomain.com/privkey.pem`
12. Click **Create** to save.

> **Important:** Your CDN Domain MUST be **Proxy ON (Orange Cloud)** in Cloudflare, and SSL/TLS mode must be set to **Full (Strict)**.

---

## 3. Hysteria 2 (Best for Poor Networks)
Hysteria uses UDP to drastically improve speeds on unstable or slow networks.

**Steps in 3x-ui Panel:**
1. Go to **Inbounds** -> Click `+ Add Inbound`.
2. **Remark:** `Hysteria_Node`.
3. **Protocol:** `hysteria2`.
4. **Port:** `457` (Ensure this UDP port is open in UFW: `ufw allow 457/udp`).
5. **Client -> Password:** (Auto-generated or enter your own).
6. Go to the **Stream Settings** tab.
7. **SNI:** `amigos.yourdomain.com` (Your Panel Domain).
8. **Certificate:** Use the paths generated by the script:
    * **Public Key Path:** `/root/cert/amigos.yourdomain.com/fullchain.pem`
    * **Private Key Path:** `/root/cert/amigos.yourdomain.com/privkey.pem`
9. Click **Create** to save.

> **Client App Note:** When adding this node to apps like v2rayNG or v2box, you may need to enable **`Allow Insecure`** in the client settings to prevent timeout issues.

---

## 4. Shadowsocks (Classic & Lightweight)
A lightweight protocol, good as a backup option.

**Steps in 3x-ui Panel:**
1. Go to **Inbounds** -> Click `+ Add Inbound`.
2. **Remark:** `Shadowsocks_Node`.
3. **Protocol:** `shadowsocks`.
4. **Port:** `39301` (Ensure this port is open in your firewall).
5. **Client -> Email:** `user1`.
6. **Encryption Method:** `aes-256-gcm` or `chacha20-ietf-poly1305` (Recommended).
7. **Password:** (Auto-generated).
8. Click **Create** to save.

---

## 📌 General Tips
* **How to get the Subscription Link/QR Code:** Click the QR code icon next to any created inbound to get the `vless://` or `hy2://` link to paste into your client app.
* **Firewall Ports:** If a node says "Timeout", first check if the port is open using `ufw status` on your server.
* **Combined Subscription:** To merge all protocols into a single link for one user, give the user the exact same `Client ID (UUID)` and `Subscription name (Remark)` across all inbounds.
